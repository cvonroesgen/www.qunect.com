Quickbase to Excel Best Practices

How to use a SQL statement and a connection string to isolate your Excel spreadsheet from Quickbase field name changes. Reduce the number of fields you bring into Excel to only those that you require for your analysis or presentation. This can dramatically speed up your refresh times. Facilitate the sharing of your spreadsheet within your organization so that your colleagues can refresh the Quickbase data with only entering your Quickbase username when prompted for credentials. A trial or perpetual license is required for QuNect ODBC for QuickBase 2021 or later.
