Application Note for QuNect ODBC for QuickBase

How to Find the DBID of a Quickbase Table

You can find the DBID of your Quickbase table by looking in the address bar of your browser when you're looking at any page of the table. The home page of all Quickbase applications have a DBID that does not refer to any of the tables in the application. You must navigate to a form or view that is specific to your table to cause the proper dbid to appear in your browser's address bar. The DBID is after the "https://yourcompany.quickbase.com/db/" and before the first question mark "?" if there is one. If there is no question mark in the URL the DBID is everything after the "https://yourcompany.quickbase.com/db/" For example the dbid in the following URL:

            https://yourcompany.quickbase.com/db/6mztyxu8?act=er&rid=20

is

            6mztyxu8
