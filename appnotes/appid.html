Application Note for QuNect ODBC for QuickBase

How to Find the APPID of a Quickbase Application

You can find the APPID of your Quickbase application by looking in the address bar of your browser when you're at the home page of your Quickbase application. Make sure you're on the home page of your application by clicking on the name of your application just below the menu bar on the left side of your screen. The APPID is after the "https://yourcompany.quickbase.com/db/". For example the appid in the following URL:

            https://yourcompany.quickbase.com/db/bcks8a7y3

is

            bcks8a7y3
