Application Note for QuNect ODBC for QuickBase

How to Find the FID of a Quickbase Field

You can find the FID of your Quickbase field by clicking on the Settings link on your application's Home page. Then click on Tables. Then on the left, click on the name of the table in question. Then scroll through the fields until you find the field in question. The fifth column over is called FIELD ID. If this column is not present, click on the button below the field list called SHOW FIELD IDS. This column contains the field identifier FID of the field.
